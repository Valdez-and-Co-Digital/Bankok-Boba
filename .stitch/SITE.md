# Bangkok Boba & Ramen Bar Web Experience

## 1. Vision & Overview
High-energy, Cyber-Thai street food web application featuring hand-crafted boba, build-your-own ramen, fresh steamed buns, and Oreo series specials for Bangkok Boba & Ramen Bar (Pleasanton, TX).

## 2. Project Metadata
- **Stitch Project ID**: `11425011211082366409`
- **Design System**: Cyber-Thai Street Neon (Glassmorphic Cards & Neon Accents)
- **Target Device**: Desktop & Mobile Responsive

- [x] **Desktop Interactive Ramen Builder** (`58551e228c5d4206a07b809b3806204d`) — Dedicated 3-step noodle, protein & veggie builder screen.
