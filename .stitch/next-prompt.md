An extended full digital menu page for Bangkok Boba & Ramen Bar featuring category filters (Boba, Ramen, Steamed Buns, Appetizers, Desserts), search, and item detail modals.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- **Theme**: Cyber-Thai Street Food (Dark Mode, Neon Glassmorphism)
- **Colors**: Canvas `#121414`, Surface Purple `#2A0E47`, Primary Pink `#ff4e7a`, Neon Green `#00E676`, Tertiary Gold `#f1c100`
- **Typography**: Montserrat (Headlines), Space Grotesk (Labels & Prices), Hanken Grotesk (Body)
- **Mobile Nav**: Fixed 72px bottom bar with standing-out floating center Order button

**Page Structure:**
1. Top Navigation Bar with brand logo & live open badge
2. Category Filter Pills (All, Boba Tea, Oreo Series, Ramen, Appetizers)
3. Grid of glassmorphic menu cards with high-res photography, tags, and prices
4. Search bar & dietary filter tags (Vegetarian, Gluten-Free, Chef's Choice)
5. Footer & mobile bottom navigation
